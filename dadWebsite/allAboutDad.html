Ron and Lisa

In a life filled with creativity, love, and unwavering dedication, our beloved dad, Ronald Arthur Huston, left an indelible mark on all who knew him. Born on September 10th, 1954, in Alexandria, Virginia, he embarked on a journey that showcased the essence of his character.

At just 17, he fearlessly joined the United States Army, serving his country with honor until the age of 19. In 1980, fate led him to a first date with the love of his life, Lisa Henningsen, and their bond grew into a timeless love story. Together, they welcomed the joys of parenthood, raising their four children the best they could. An incredibly hardworking man, Ronald embraced the role of provider wholeheartedly, working long hours to ensure his family's happiness and comfort. In 2002, he became a devoted stay-at-home dad, and with his boundless talents, he founded "The Restoration Wizard", a carpet cleaning business. Ronald's creative spirit shone through his passion for woodworking, chime-making, and restoring old cars. Throughout his life, Ronald's heart overflowed with love and compassion. He adopted numerous dogs throughout his lifetime, all whose presence brought immense joy and comfort to him. In 2016, he found solace in faith, accepting Jesus Christ as his Lord and Savior, and on February 14th of that same year, he took a momentous step in his spiritual journey as he was baptized.

Surrounded by his adoring family members, Ronald's journey on this earth reached its conclusion on June 25th, 2023, at the age of 68. His legacy of love, resilience, and creative spirit lives on in the cherished memories he leaves behind. As we bid farewell to this extraordinary man, we find comfort in the knowledge that his love and influence will forever resonate in our hearts. Rest in peace, dear dad, knowing that your impact on our lives will never fade.

"When we have children, we want them to grow up with everything we didn't have. We want them to experience everything we never had a chance to. We want them to be stronger than us in every way. My father did all of that and more. He did everything in his power to instill respect and discipline in us. There were times when he pushed us to our limits. But he had another side that he hardly showed. It was that of loving, caring, and compassion. All that has led me to where I am today. I can be as cold as ice, but twice as nice. Without my mentor, I feel lost. But as long as we pass on the values and beliefs he instilled in me I believe that his legacy will live on. I will never forget one moment, not one line he said to me. He was not just my father. He was a mentor. He was a family man. He was a provider. He was a care giver. He was one hell of a unique man. He was my best friend. I am honored to have called him Dad."
-SpaC3CaD3t
